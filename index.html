About

I'm a Senior Software Engineer with 7+ years of experience building web applications with Ruby on Rails and React. I'm drawn to work where technology serves a meaningful purpose, whether that's improving healthcare outcomes, supporting climate action, or increasing financial accessibility. I studied Environmental Engineering at Rice University and am based in Minneapolis.

Most recently at Tripleseat, I led the adoption of modern React practices and test-driven development across our Rails applications. Before that, I built healthcare software at Icario and developed internal tools at Modis. I'm passionate about mentoring teammates, establishing sustainable coding practices, and architecting projects with quality at their core.
